AMITY HAYZE // EPK

Transmission: Bio

Amity Hayze (pronounced AH-muh-tee HAYZ) is a songwriter, producer, and multi-instrumentalist blending the raw intimacy of Midwest emo with the hazy textures of shoegaze and alternative indie rock. From rainy cabins in rural Oregon to DIY stages in LA, she builds immersive worlds where music, visuals, and storytelling collide.

Hayze's work has been featured in The Under Magazine Issue 1 (2024) and Issue 2 (2025) as well as Queer Out Here Issue 10 (2026) and BloodLust Magazine (2025). She has also been featured in various blogs, her single "How Could You Love Me?" recently being featured on "Wishful Thinking Press (2025). Her work has been spotlighted on multiple Spotify playlists curated by independent music connoisseurs as well as being featured on (and the image cover of "Selected by Amuse" (2026).
