Overview

Amity Hayze is a self-produced alternative artist blending washed-out guitar textures, tambourine snaps, and intimate lyricism. Re-emerging from a prior project (SXREY), Amity records music that feels like a private transmission — equal parts nostalgia and urgency. Her music has gained organic traction through short-form video edits, grassroots sticker drops, and a DIY aesthetic.

Bio (Short)

Amity started making music as a way to hold language for feelings that felt too big for words. Growing up mixed (Native, white, Mexican), she rewrote the soundtrack she wanted to hear — a blend of shoegaze haze, Midwestern emo honesty, and home-recorded warmth. Her music is for 2am headphones, burnt CD binders, and anyone who wants something that feels like home.
