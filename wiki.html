Amity Hayze

Origin: Torrance,CA, U.S.

Genres: Alternative, Surfer Rock, Shoegaze, Midwest Emo

Years active: 2020 – present

Former alias: SXREY

Amity Hayze (stylized in static transmissions as "Hayze") is an American multi-instrumentalist, producer, and visual artist. Her work fuses surfer rock guitar tones, shoegaze textures, and Midwest emo lyricism, all recorded and produced independently in Los Angeles.

Background

Hayze began creating music under the alias SXREY, cultivating a small audience before fully re-emerging as Amity Hayze in 2025. The project emphasizes DIY ethos, analog aesthetics, and hidden transmissions in media — including VHS visuals, CRT distortion, and coded easter eggs in her website.

Music

Her debut single, "Gone is the Time" (2025), was recorded in a rural Oregon cabin during a rainstorm. The track reflects themes of lost potential, time, and self-worth, while capturing a nostalgic, atmospheric sound.
