Amity Hayze

Amity Hayze is an independent alternative artist blending surfer rock, shoegaze, and Midwest emo influences. She is known for self-producing her music and visuals, creating a DIY aesthetic that incorporates VHS textures, CRT visuals, and 360° video editing.

Early Life

Although a born Californian, Hayze began recording music in Oregon, where her surroundings and the Pacific Northwest weather influenced her sound. She has roots in Native American, Mexican, and white heritage, which inform her perspective and identity as an artist.

Music Career

Before performing as Amity Hayze, she released music under the name SXREY for four years, gradually developing her sound and building a small audience. In 2025, she began releasing fully self-produced work under her alias "Amity Hayze" where she battles her struggles of self worth and image through alternative tones .
