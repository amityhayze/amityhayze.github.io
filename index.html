About

Amity Hayze writes from the static — mixing washed-out guitar tones, haunting vocals, and honest late-night lyrics. A DIY producer who records in a cabin, Amity’s work blends nostalgia and immediacy into short transmissions meant for headphones at 2am.

Started out as SXREY and re-emerged as Amity Hayze in 2025 to release new fully produced material.
