About

Amity Hayze writes from the static — mixing washed-out guitar tones, haunting vocals, and honest late-night lyrics. As a multi-instrumentalist producer who records in LA, Amity’s work blends nostalgia and immediacy into short transmissions meant for headphones at 2am.
